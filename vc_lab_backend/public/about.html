About the Center of Excellence in Visual Computing

The Centre of Competence in Visual Computing provides computing facilities for students, research scholars and faculty members. The objective of the centre is to bring the students and faculty of various disciplines together to execute interdisciplinary projects. The Centre facilitates execution of computationally intensive research works in various state-of-the-art domains including Edge Computing, Parallel Programming, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The centre offers internships, trainings and facilitate skill enhancement in the areas like Image/Video analytics, Mobile Application Development, Internet of Things, Natural Language Processing

Key Areas of Focus

  • Food, Nutrition Environment and Agriculture
  • Machining and Novel view synthesis
  • Remote sensing and Geo Informatics
  • Remote sensing and Geo Informatics
